What is a Persian Daff drum?

The Persian Daff drum is a traditional frame drum originating from Iran, typically made with a wooden frame and a goatskin head. It is commonly used in Persian music and Sufi rituals.

What sizes are available for the Persian Daff drums?

Our online store offers Persian Daff drums in various sizes ranging from small to large, catering to different preferences and playing styles.

Are the Persian Daff drums handcrafted?

Yes, all our Persian Daff drums are handcrafted by skilled artisans with attention to detail and traditional techniques, ensuring high quality and authenticity.

Do you offer customization options for the Persian Daff drums?

We provide customization options for our Persian Daff drums, allowing customers to choose specific designs, materials, and sizes to suit their preferences.

How can I care for my Persian Daff drum?

To maintain the quality and longevity of your Persian Daff drum, we recommend storing it in a dry place, avoiding direct sunlight or extreme temperatures, and periodically conditioning the goatskin head.
